Introduction

As everyone knows, driving a project requires determination, perseverance and unfailing commitment. Among these great qualities, one word barely rates a mention... This word doesn't produce such a democratizing and federating effect. However, it makes all the difference between an imaginative person, unable to act on his ideas and a creative person who will bring them to life.

Call it how you want, expertise, know-how, technical ability... KNOWLEDGE is an essential part of the creative process.

During 15 years, I've worked as a graphic designer, then art director in advertising agencies. Meanwhile, I constantly tried to raise my competences to accomplish the best with the resources at my disposal.
