Who I Am
and What I Do.

I'm Gatien GREGORI, a highly motivated chap with more than fifteen years experience in Communication, Design and Development. I've worked with several larger as well as smaller brands and I'm a fan of challenging projects. Check out my experience, my work and let's get in touch.

For every project that I have done my focus has been on creating the best user experience possible. I'm both a designer and a developer which means that I take charge of the entire process, from conception to design, to development, to testing.
